Fast Scan 2 PDF

Privacy Policy

Fast Scan 2 PDF does not collect, store, or share your personal documents. All scanned documents and generated PDF files remain stored locally on your device.

The app uses your camera only for scanning documents. Images are processed locally and are never uploaded to our servers.

Advertising

This app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google AdMob may collect limited device information, such as device identifiers, to provide and improve advertising in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

The app does not use:

  • User accounts
  • Cloud storage
  • Cross-app tracking

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit:
https://policies.google.com/privacy
