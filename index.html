Fast Scan 2 PDF

Privacy Policy

Fast Scan 2 PDF does not collect, store, or share personal data.

All scanned documents and generated PDFs remain locally on your device.

The app does not use:

  • User accounts
  • Cloud storage
  • Analytics
  • Tracking
  • Third-party advertising

Camera access is only used to scan documents directly on your device.
